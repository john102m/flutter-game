# Game Rules (Quick Reference)

## Core Mechanics
- 6 companies, each with a **parent peg** (share price, rows 12–32) and a **traveller peg** (action zone, rows 2–11).
- **Two dice:** colour die (picks company 0–5), numbered die (1–6, moves traveller UP).
- **Buy/sell:** ONLY immediately before your dice throw. £5 brokerage to buy, free to sell. 100-share certificates only.
- Players start with £300. Win at £600 total capital (cash + shares at market price).

## Board Layout (32 rows, top to bottom)
- Rows 2–11: Action zone (20%, SLUMP, 10%, 10%, SLUMP, 10%, 5%, 5%, 5%, M)
- Rows 12–32: Price zone (£200 down to BANKRUPT)
- PAR is row 22 (£100) — starting position for all parent pegs.
- Travellers start on their parent peg and move UP through the action zone.

## Round End
Triggered when ANY traveller reaches/passes the top (row 2). ALL companies assessed:

| Traveller Position | Dividend/100 shares | Parent Peg |
|---|---|---|
| Top (20%) | £20 | +2 up |
| Opposite 10% | £10 | +1 up |
| Opposite 5% | £5 | +1 up |
| Opposite Slump (with anti-slump) | £10 | +1 up |
| Opposite Market News | £0 | No move |
| Below Market News | £0 | −1 down |
| Still on Parent Peg | £0 | −2 down |

After processing: travellers return to parent pegs, Market News reshuffled.

## Special Events
- **SLUMP:** Traveller drops back 6 spaces.
- **M / Market News:** Draw top card, apply effect to that company only.
- **Bonus Shares:** Parent peg hits £200 → 1-for-1 bonus, parent returns to PAR 100.
- **Bankruptcy:** Parent peg hits bottom → company removed.

## Win Condition
First player to £600+ total capital. Claims ONLY during dividend processing. Highest total wins ties.
